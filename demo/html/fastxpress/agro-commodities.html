Agro Commodities

Our Business

At Oceanda, we specialize in physical commodity trading, particularly in high-quality seafood sourced from Africa (Nigeria, Ghana, Cameroon, Ivory Coast). We deal in a wide range of seafood products, including shrimp, lobster, tilapia, catfish, mackerel, and crabs, all sustainably harvested from local waters. Our seafood is naturally farmed or caught, ensuring it meets non-GMO standards and is free of harmful chemicals. We source, process, and export seafood in large volumes, meeting the exacting demands of our global clients.

Agro Commodities Products

Maize

Maize, also known as corn, is a staple crop used globally for food, feed, and industrial purposes. It is processed into flour, syrup, ethanol, and other products and is a key commodity in both human consumption and livestock farming

Cassava

Cassava is a root crop widely grown in tropical regions. It is a major source of carbohydrates and can be processed into various products like cassava flour, tapioca, and starch, which are used in food production and industry.

Honey

Honey is a natural sweetener produced by bees from the nectar of flowers. It is valued for its medicinal properties, culinary uses, and as a natural alternative to processed sugars.

Kolanut

Kolanut is traditionally chewed in many West African cultures for its stimulant properties, thanks to its caffeine content. It is also used in the beverage industry and has cultural and medicinal significance in many regions.

Cocoa beans

Cocoa beans are the seeds of the cacao tree and the primary raw material used in chocolate production. They are highly valued for their rich flavor and are widely cultivated in West African countries like Nigeria and Ghana. Cocoa is also used in beverages, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals.

Sesame seeds

Sesame seeds are known for their high oil content and are used in a variety of culinary applications, including baking, oil extraction, and as a topping for various foods. They are also rich in essential nutrients such as calcium, magnesium, and zinc.

Cashew

Cashew nuts are highly nutritious and popular as a snack or ingredient in many dishes. Apart from the edible nut, the cashew apple and shell oil are used in various industries, including food, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals.

SPICES (ginger, garlic, tumeric, thyme, mint, bayleaves)

These spices are essential in both culinary and medicinal applications. Ginger and turmeric are known for their anti-inflammatory properties, garlic for its health benefits, and thyme, mint, and bay leaves for their aromatic qualities in cooking.

Shea Butter

Shea butter is a fat extracted from the nuts of the shea tree, native to Africa. It is widely used in cosmetics, skincare products, and as a cooking ingredient due to its moisturizing and anti-inflammatory properties.
