About Us

About Us

At Oceanda, we are committed to excellence in the sourcing and export of premium commodities. Specializing in both soft and hard products, we deliver a diverse range of high-quality minerals, agro commodities, and seafood to clients across the globe. Our extensive reach spans Africa and beyond, making us a trusted partner in the international trade sector. With a strong network of reliable suppliers and a team of industry experts, Oceanda ensures that every product meets the highest standards of quality. We take pride in our seamless logistics operations, providing efficient, timely, and secure shipments to meet the unique needs of our clients. From the sourcing of top-tier commodities to consulting services and cloud technology solutions, Oceanda is at the forefront of innovation and reliability in the global market.

OUR BUSINESS

Agricultural products

At Oceanda, we specialize in physical commodity trading, particularly in high-quality seafood sourced from Africa (Nigeria, Ghana, Cameroon, Ivory Coast). We deal in a wide range of seafood products, including shrimp, lobster, tilapia, catfish, mackerel, and crabs, all sustainably harvested from local waters. Our seafood is naturally farmed or caught, ensuring it meets non-GMO standards and is free of harmful chemicals. We source, process, and export seafood in large volumes, meeting the exacting demands of our global clients.

Solid Minerals

At Oceanda, we focus on the extraction, processing, and export of high-quality solid minerals from Africa (Nigeria, Ghana, Cameroon, Ivory Coast). Our commitment to excellence ensures that the minerals we source are of top-grade quality, meeting global standards for various industrial applications. From rare earth elements like lithium, essential in modern technology, to traditional commodities like coal and iron ore, which fuel global infrastructure, we provide a reliable supply chain for solid minerals.

Cloud Solution

At Oceanda, we specialize in providing cloud solutions that enhance operational efficiency and drive innovation across various sectors. Our offerings include cloud migration, infrastructure management, and data analytics, all designed to meet the evolving needs of our clients. We ensure secure and scalable cloud environments, enabling businesses to leverage cutting-edge technology while optimizing performance and reducing costs. Our team is dedicated to delivering customized solutions that align with our clients' strategic goals, empowering them to thrive in a digital landscape.
